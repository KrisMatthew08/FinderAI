About FinderAI

FinderAI is an innovative lost and found management system designed specifically for Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao. Using advanced AI-powered image recognition technology, we help reunite lost items with their rightful owners quickly and efficiently.

What We Do

AI-Powered Matching

Our advanced AI analyzes images and descriptions to automatically match lost and found items with high accuracy.

Fast Processing

Get instant notifications when potential matches are found for your lost or found items.

Secure & Private

Your personal information and item details are protected with industry-standard security measures.

Community-Driven

Built for the Mapúa MCM community, making it easy for students and staff to help each other.

Easy to Use

Simple interface that works on any device - just upload a photo and provide details.

Track Progress

Monitor your submissions and see matched items through your personal dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a found item?

1. Log in to your FinderAI account
2. Go to the home page and select "Report Found Item"
3. Upload a clear photo of the item
4. Select the category and provide details
5. Submit the form and wait for potential matches

How does the AI matching work?

Our AI analyzes the images and descriptions you provide to find potential matches. It uses advanced computer vision to compare visual features and text descriptions. When a potential match is found, both parties receive notifications.

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, you need to create an account to submit found or lost items. This helps us maintain security and allows you to track your submissions and receive notifications about potential matches.

What should I do if I get a match notification?

When you receive a match notification, review the details carefully. If it looks like your item, you can initiate contact through the platform. For found items, coordinate a safe meetup location on campus to return the item.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and only shared when necessary for item recovery. We follow strict data protection protocols to keep your information secure.

How long are items kept in the system?

Items remain active in the system for 90 days. After this period, if no match is found, the listing may be archived. You can always resubmit if needed.

Can I edit my submission after posting?

Currently, you cannot edit submissions directly. If you need to make changes, please contact support or delete and resubmit your item with the correct information.

What types of items can I report?

You can report any lost or found item including wallets, phones, keys, bags, ID cards, electronics, jewelry, and other personal belongings commonly found on campus.

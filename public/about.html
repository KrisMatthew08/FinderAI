About FinderAI

FinderAI is an innovative lost and found management system designed specifically for Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao. Using advanced AI-powered image recognition technology, we help reunite lost items with their rightful owners quickly and efficiently.

What We Do

AI-Powered Matching

Our advanced machine learning algorithms analyze images to find the best matches between lost and found items.

Instant Results

Get matched with potential items in seconds. No more waiting days to find your belongings.

Secure & Private

Your data is protected with industry-standard security. Only verified students can access the system.

Easy to Use

Simple interface designed for students. Just upload a photo and let our AI do the rest.

Real-Time Notifications

Get instantly notified when someone claims your found item or when a potential match is found.

Smart Analytics

Track patterns and statistics to improve campus security and item recovery rates.

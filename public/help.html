Help & FAQs

Everything you need to know about using FinderAI

How do I report a lost or found item?

Simply click on "FIND NOW" on the homepage or scroll to the report section. Choose whether you found an item or lost one, upload a clear photo, fill in the details (category, location, description), and submit. Our AI will automatically start matching your item! Make sure to provide accurate information for better matching results.

How does the AI matching work?

Our advanced AI analyzes the image you upload, extracting features like color, texture, shape, and patterns. It then compares these features with other items in the database using cosine similarity. The system also considers the category (+15% bonus for same category), date proximity (up to +10% for items reported within 7 days), and description similarity (up to +10% for matching keywords) to provide the most accurate matches possible. Items with a match score of 50% or higher will appear in your potential matches.

Will I be notified when a match is found?

Yes! You'll receive real-time notifications in your dashboard when someone reports an item that matches yours. The notification bell icon in the navigation bar will show a badge with the number of unread notifications. Make sure to check your notifications regularly for the best chance of recovering your item.

How do I claim a matched item?

When you see a match in your dashboard, click "This is My Item!" to claim it. The system will notify the person who found your item through the notification system. You'll need to coordinate with them or visit the Lost & Found Desk to retrieve your item. Please bring a valid ID (student ID preferred) and be prepared to verify ownership by providing additional details about the item.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Only registered Mapúa MCM students can access the system using their student ID and password. Personal information is never shared publicly, and only relevant parties (the person who found your item or lost the item you found) will receive notifications. Your privacy and security are our top priorities. All data is stored securely in encrypted databases.

What if I don't find a match?

Don't worry! Your item stays in the system, and you'll be notified if a match appears later. You can also try uploading different photos from various angles or updating your description with more details. Sometimes items are reported days or weeks later, so keep checking back. For immediate assistance, visit the Lost & Found Desk on campus or contact us directly through our contact page.

What kind of photo should I upload?

For best results, upload a clear, well-lit photo of the item. Make sure the item is in focus and takes up most of the frame. Avoid blurry images, extreme close-ups, or photos with lots of background clutter. If possible, take the photo against a plain background. The better the photo quality, the more accurate the AI matching will be!

Can I dismiss a match if it's not my item?

Yes! If you see a potential match that isn't your item, simply click the "Not a Match" button. This will remove it from your current matches and help improve the system's accuracy. The match will be recorded as dismissed and won't show up again in your dashboard.
